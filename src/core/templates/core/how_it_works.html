{% trans "How Web-4D-QSAR works" %}

The creation of pharmacos with the aid of computing is a research area of great importance in chemistry and related fields.

The toolkit is available for this purpose, among others, in programs for generating descriptors and construction and validation of mathematical models in QSAR (English, Quantitative Structure-Activity Relationship). Aiming to make this more accessible to the scientific community, new methodologies and programs were developed (MARTINS, 2013) study. Including a new 4D QSAR methodology, known as LQTA-QSAR, whose goal is to generate spatial descriptors taking into account the profiles of conformational sampling of molecules under study obtained from molecular dynamics simulations.

Based on these methodologies developed, a Web platform was created to provide an integrated environment for performing molecular dynamics and generation of system descriptors.
